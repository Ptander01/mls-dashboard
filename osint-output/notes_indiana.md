## Development Timeline
- 2023: Land acquired via Razor5 LLC shell company
- April 9, 2024: Tax abatement documents reveal possible $11B Amazon data center
- April 25, 2024: Governor Holcomb officially announces AWS $11B investment
- March 2024: Indiana council votes to rezone agricultural land for data center
- August 14, 2024: Final incentive approvals from St. Joseph County
- October 2024: Construction underway
- May 2025: All 16 planned buildings under construction (satellite imagery)
- October 29, 2025: Amazon opens data center (Project Rainier operational)
- November 24, 2025: Amazon announces additional $15B investment in Northern Indiana
- March 3, 2026: Dewatering request for southwest corner
